Hypothesis Testing

Using the Scipy library, I carry out a statistics test to find out if the data between the Median Household Income and the Median Household Rent is significant. I use a T-Test of Independece which is the test used for the means of two independent samples. The null hypothesis in my study is that the Median Household Income and the Median Household Rent have identical average values and I'm hoping to either reject or accept it based off the results of my p-value.A p-value helps you determine the significance of your results when you perform a hypothesis test.If we reject the null hypothesis, the results are considered significant.

In [16]:
ttest,pval = ttest_ind(income['value'], rent['value'])
print("The p-value is",pval)

if pval < 0.05:
  print("We reject the null hypothesis")
else:
  print("We accept the null hypothesis")

The p-value is 1.779090659893951e-16
